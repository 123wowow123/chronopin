About

Chronopin, is a social event bookmarking app for organizing, sharing and discovering time relevant content and treats chronology as a first class citizen via it's timeline user interface. It also leverages the latest generation of AI models to suggest user relevant pins and powers it's natural language searching capabilities.

Released in 2023, the service is available via mobile and desktop web browsers.

Functionality

Chronopin allows the user to chronologically pin events or web page to remote servers for later reading and to share with others. Users can add notes, tags, monetary costs and allows easy chronological tracking of timely contents in a infinite scroll timeline user interface.

Chronopin empowers serendipitous event discovery and other time relavant occurrences. Users can track release dates, plan travels, or just plan their day. Through the social nature of the app, event or trips can be planned and promoted with the help of the community through comment discussions, ranging from but not limited to, tips for the type of gear, lodging locations, and type of transport to make the trip more enjoyable.

Crowdsourced knowledge can be leveraged to narrow down ambiguous event dates and sentiments.

Chronopin hopes to help users better navigate the world that has evolved at an ever increasing pace and gives a prescient view of what's to come.
